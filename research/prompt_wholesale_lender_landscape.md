## Prompt

Non-QM/DSCR Wholesale Lender Landscape: Program Comparison, State Licensing, and Broker Channel Intelligence

I'm building a B2B lead generation platform that sells scored investor leads to mortgage loan officers who originate DSCR loans. I need to understand the wholesale lender ecosystem they work within so I can speak their language, understand their pain points, and position my product effectively.

### Part 1: Wholesale DSCR Lender Directory

Identify every major wholesale lender that offers DSCR loan programs to mortgage brokers as of 2025-2026. For each lender provide:

- Company name
- DSCR program name (if branded)
- Minimum DSCR ratio accepted (e.g., 0.75x, 1.0x, 1.25x)
- Maximum LTV
- Minimum FICO score
- Property types allowed (SFR, 2-4 unit, 5-8 unit, condos, condotels, STR, mixed-use)
- Interest-only option availability
- Rate range or typical spread over benchmark
- Geographic restrictions or state licensing footprint (which states can they lend in?)
- Whether they accept LLC/entity vesting
- Loan amount range (min and max)
- Typical turn time (days from submission to close)
- Whether they accept AirDNA or STR income for qualification

Key lenders to cover (but don't limit to these): Angel Oak Mortgage Solutions, Deephaven Mortgage, A&D Mortgage, Newfi Wholesale, Griffin Funding, Carrington Mortgage, RCN Capital, Lima One Capital, Kiavi, Visio Lending, Easy Street Capital, Civic Financial Services, CoreVest Finance, Foundation CREF, Velocity Mortgage Capital, Truss Financial Group, Arc Home, Verus Mortgage Capital, Logan Finance, Change Wholesale, NQM Funding, Acra Lending, Roc360/Roc Capital, MortgageRight, Planet Financial Group, Sprout Mortgage (if still active), First National Bank of America.

### Part 2: State Licensing Map for DSCR Origination

- Which states require broker company licensing to originate DSCR loans?
- Which states require individual MLO licensing?
- Which states have additional requirements beyond standard NMLS (loan broker registration, real estate license, specific bond amounts)?
- Which states restrict or prohibit DSCR origination entirely?
- Which states classify DSCR/investment property loans as business-purpose (exempt from ATR/QM/TILA consumer protections) vs. states that may treat certain investor loans as consumer-purpose?
- Are there states where the business-purpose exemption is contested or where state-level consumer lending laws still apply even for investment properties?

### Part 3: Broker Channel Dynamics

- What percentage of total DSCR origination volume flows through the wholesale/broker channel vs. retail (direct lender) vs. correspondent?
- How do mortgage brokers typically discover and evaluate DSCR wholesale lender partners? (trade shows, wholesaler AE outreach, online rate comparisons, peer referral?)
- What are the most common pain points brokers report when originating DSCR loans? (guideline changes, appraisal disputes, rent schedule methodology, turn times, stacking requirements, reserve calculations, insurance requirements)
- What compensation do brokers typically earn on DSCR originations? (basis points on loan amount, typical range, how does this compare to conventional originations?)
- What is the typical DSCR loan size by market tier? (e.g., Midwest cash-flow markets vs. coastal appreciation markets)
- What are the most common reasons DSCR loans fall out of pipeline (fail to close)?

### Part 4: Competitive Intelligence — Existing DSCR Lead Providers

Who are the existing companies that sell DSCR-related leads, investor leads, or property data to loan officers? For each competitor provide:

- Company name and URL
- What they sell (raw property data, skip-traced contacts, scored leads, intent signals, aged leads)
- Pricing model (per lead, monthly subscription, per record)
- Typical price per lead or monthly cost
- Data freshness and sourcing methodology
- Whether leads are exclusive or shared
- Customer reviews or reputation in the LO community

Companies to cover: Aged Lead Store, PropStream, BatchData, Reonomy, Privy, InvestorFuse, REsimpli, BatchLeads, DealMachine, RealQuest (CoreLogic), ListSource (CoreLogic), DataTree (First American), PropertyRadar, Forecasa, SmartZip, Offrs, Catalyze AI, Landvoice.

Where are the gaps in what these providers offer? Specifically:
- Do any of them provide ICP scoring (ranking leads by likelihood of needing DSCR financing)?
- Do any provide entity resolution (LLC to real person)?
- Do any provide financing intelligence (current lender, rate, maturity, equity, refi signals)?
- Do any provide AI-generated talking points?
- Do any offer geographic exclusivity?
