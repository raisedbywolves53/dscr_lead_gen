# DSCR Lead Intelligence — Pricing Model

## Two Programs

### Program 1: Deal Intelligence Platform
The LO gets scored investor dossiers with verified contact data and personalized talking points. They do their own outreach.

### Program 2: Done-For-You Outbound Engine
We run multi-channel digital campaigns (email sequences, direct mail, LinkedIn outreach) using the same intelligence. The LO gets warm responses and booked calls.

## Fraud Protection — Outcome Verification

### Problem
If any pricing component is tied to funded deals, the LO can under-report closings.

### Solution: Multi-Layer Verification

**1. Subscription-first pricing (guaranteed revenue)**
- Base fee is non-negotiable — covers data + campaigns
- Performance bonus is upside, not the whole model
- Even if they under-report, we're profitable on the base

**2. Public record monitoring (automated verification)**
- County clerk / Register of Deeds records new mortgages within 30-60 days
- We cross-reference: new mortgage on a property we delivered as a lead + lender matches our LO's company = verified close
- Scripts 11/16 in the pipeline already scrape these sources
- "We noticed a new mortgage recorded on 1327 Lee St — congrats! Performance bonus applies."
- They cannot hide a funded deal — it's public record

**3. Program 2 — we own the funnel**
- Emails sent from our infrastructure → we see every reply
- Direct mail has our tracking number/URL → we see inbound
- LinkedIn messages from managed accounts → we see conversations
- Warm responses handed off via documented warm introduction
- Top-of-funnel activity is fully visible to us

**4. Contractual protections**
- Right to audit clause
- 12-month lead attribution window
- Market exclusivity (can't claim another source for same investor)
- Self-reporting with quarterly reconciliation against public records

- $500 one-time pilot: 100 Tier 1 dossiers in their market
- Use the sales demo sheet to pitch
- Target: Raleigh/Charlotte LOs actively doing DSCR volume

### Phase 2: Convert to Program 1 (Starter)
- Pilot proves data quality → $1,500/mo subscription
- Monthly refresh, expanded county coverage

### Phase 3: Upsell to Program 2 (Launch)
- "You're only calling 20% of leads — let us run email + direct mail"
- $3,500/mo, we own the outbound channel

### Phase 4: Add Performance Bonus
- Build public record monitoring (mortgage recording alerts)
- $500-1,000 bonus per verified funded deal
- Verified via county clerk records, not honor system
