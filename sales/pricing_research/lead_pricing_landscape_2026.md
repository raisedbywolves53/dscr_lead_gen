# DSCR & Real Estate Investor Lead Pricing Landscape
**Research Date:** March 17, 2026
**Purpose:** Competitive pricing intelligence for positioning our lead product

---

## Executive Summary

The mortgage/RE investor lead market has a wide pricing spectrum: from $0.02/record for raw skip-traced data up to $150/lead for live-transfer qualified prospects. There is **no established player selling "DSCR-specific" leads** as a named product category — this is a gap in the market. Most lead sellers focus on refinance, purchase, or reverse mortgage leads segmented by credit tier, not by loan product type (conventional vs DSCR vs hard money). The closest analog is "real estate investor leads" sold by DIY data platforms, but these are raw property lists, not enriched + scored leads ready for outreach.

### Key Takeaways from MortgageLeads.com
- Shared leads are roughly 40-50% of exclusive pricing
- Credit quality creates a 2-3x price spread
- Live transfers command a 3-4x premium over standard exclusive leads
- These are consumer-initiated leads (people filling out forms), NOT data-mined investor lists

### Other Mortgage Lead Sellers (Contact-for-Pricing Models)
Most major mortgage lead companies do NOT publish pricing:
- **LendingTree** — pay-per-lead marketplace, custom pricing per lender
- **Zillow (Premier Agent)** — advertising spend model, not per-lead pricing; reportedly $20-$60/lead depending on market
- **Credible** — lender partner program, custom pricing
- **LeadPops** — custom pricing after questionnaire; offers exclusive, real-time, verified mortgage leads
- **Zurple** — exclusive leads, custom pricing; reportedly $200-$500/mo + ad spend

### Aged Lead Store
- **$200 minimum order**
- Aged mortgage leads (30-180+ days old) — no per-lead pricing published
- Industry standard for aged leads: **$0.50–$5.00/lead** depending on age
  - 30-day aged: ~$3-5/lead
  - 60-day aged: ~$1-3/lead
  - 90+ day aged: ~$0.50-2/lead
- Data typically includes: name, phone, email, state, loan type requested, credit range

## 2. Lead Types & Tiers (Industry Standard Categories)

### By Freshness
| Type | Description | Typical Price Range |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Real-time / Live** | Consumer just submitted inquiry | $20–$55 (form fill), $100–$150 (live transfer) |
| **Aged (30 days)** | 1 month old, may have been contacted | $3–$5 |
| **Aged (60 days)** | 2 months old | $1–$3 |
| **Aged (90+ days)** | 3+ months old | $0.50–$2 |

### By Exclusivity
| Type | Description | Price Premium |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Exclusive** | Sold to one buyer only | Full price (baseline) |
| **Shared** | Sold to 2-4 buyers | 40-60% of exclusive price |
| **Open/Recycled** | Sold to many, may be re-sold | 10-25% of exclusive price |

### By Generation Method
| Type | Description | Typical Price |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Trigger leads** | Consumer pulled credit for mortgage (from credit bureaus) | $0.15–$0.50/lead |
| **Intent leads** | Showed online intent (searched for mortgage rates, visited lender sites) | $10–$30 |
| **Form-fill leads** | Consumer completed a form requesting info | $15–$55 |
| **Live transfers** | Consumer on the phone, transferred to LO | $75–$150 |
| **Data-mined lists** | Property records + skip trace, no intent signal | $0.02–$0.50/record |

### Trigger Leads — Special Category
- Generated when a consumer's credit is pulled for a mortgage application
- Credit bureaus sell these to competing lenders
- **Extremely controversial** — considered predatory by many in the industry
- Congress has repeatedly tried to ban them (the "Protecting Consumers from Merchant Trigger Leads Act" has been introduced multiple times)
- Price: approximately **$0.15–$0.50 per lead**
- Very high volume, very low quality per individual lead
- Data: name, address, credit inquiry type — NO phone/email without skip trace

## 3. Data Points Included by Lead Tier

### Tier 1: Raw Property Data List ($0.01–$0.05/record)
- Owner name
- Property address
- Mailing address (if different)
- Property type
- Transfer date / sale price (some sources)
- Assessed value

### Tier 2: Skip-Traced Contact Data ($0.05–$0.20/record on top of Tier 1)
- Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Phone number(s) — cell, landline
- Email address(es)
- Possible relatives/associates

### Tier 3: Enriched Lead ($0.50–$5.00/record)
- Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- Mortgage details (lender, rate, balance, origination date)
- Estimated equity
- Portfolio size (number of properties owned)
- Entity ownership (LLC/Corp/Trust details)
- Rental estimates
- Credit tier indicator

### Tier 4: Qualified Lead / Full Dossier ($10–$55/lead)
- Everything in Tier 3, plus:
- Demonstrated intent (form fill, credit inquiry, or behavior signal)
- Validated contact info (phone verified, email verified)
- Loan type interest confirmed
- Self-reported credit range
- Self-reported property value and desired loan amount

### Tier 5: Live Transfer ($75–$150/lead)
- Everything in Tier 4, plus:
- Live person on the phone
- Pre-qualified to basic criteria
- 90-second buffer to accept/reject

**Key insight for pricing our product:** Our pipeline produces Tier 3 leads (enriched + scored) with some Tier 4 elements (validated contacts, portfolio analysis). This puts us in the **$2–$10/lead** range based on data depth alone, potentially $15–$25 if we add intent signals or behavioral scoring.

## 7. Market Gap: No One Sells "DSCR Leads" as a Product

After exhaustive searching, **no company specifically markets or sells "DSCR leads"** as a named product. Here's what exists:

### What's available:
- **Generic mortgage leads** (refinance, purchase, home equity) — from form-fill lead sellers
- **Real estate investor lists** — from DIY data platforms (PropStream, BatchLeads, etc.) — raw property data, not leads
- **Trigger leads** — credit pull notifications, not investor-specific
- **Hard money / fix-and-flip leads** — some niche providers, but no public pricing found

### What's NOT available:
- Pre-built lists of investment property owners scored for DSCR loan readiness
- Leads enriched with DSCR-relevant signals (portfolio size, rental income, equity position, entity ownership)
- Campaign-ready investor leads with validated contact info + property/mortgage data

### This is your competitive advantage.
The market has two extremes:
1. **Raw data** ($0.01–$0.10/record) from DIY platforms — user does all the work
2. **Consumer intent leads** ($15–$55/lead) from form-fill companies — but these are for homebuyers/refinancers, NOT investors

**Nobody occupies the middle: enriched, scored, DSCR-specific investor leads with validated contacts and property intelligence.**

## 8. Pricing Framework for Our Product

Based on this research, here's how our leads compare and what the market will bear:

### What We Produce (per lead)
- Property data (address, type, value, transfer date)
- Owner identification (individual or entity resolution)
- Entity details (LLC/Corp/Trust lookup via SoS)
- Mortgage data (lender, origination, estimated balance/equity)
- Portfolio analysis (multi-property ownership detection)
- ICP scoring (DSCR opportunity score)
- Skip-traced contacts (phone + email via Tracerfy)
- Validated contacts (phone type via Twilio, email via MillionVerifier)
- Rental estimates (via HUD)
- Wealth signals (FEC, 990s)

### Our Cost to Produce
Based on enrichment stack costs:
- Tracerfy skip trace: $0.02/match
- MillionVerifier: ~$0.0025/email
- Twilio phone validation: $0.008/lookup
- ATTOM mortgage data: ~$0.19–$1.00/record (optional)
- Public records: free (county data, FEC, IRS 990)
- **Total variable cost: ~$0.03–$0.25/lead** (depending on enrichment depth)
- Plus fixed costs: platform subscriptions, compute, labor
