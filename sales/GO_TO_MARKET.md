# DSCR Lead Intelligence — Go-To-Market Plan

## Mission
Sign 3-5 paying NC loan officers within 60 days to prove the business model.

## Target Customer
NC-based mortgage loan officers who originate DSCR / non-QM investment property loans.

**Why they buy:**
- DSCR commission: $3K-$12.5K per funded deal (1-2.5% on $300K-$500K loan)
- Repeat investor relationships = $15K-60K+ lifetime value (5+ loans per investor)
- Most LOs rely on referrals or garbage lead lists (recycled property records, no intelligence)
- Our product gives them scored, enriched investor dossiers — names, phones, emails, portfolio size, equity estimates, refi signals

**Entry offer:** $500 pilot → 100 Tier 1 dossiers for their county
**Breakeven for LO:** One closed deal from pilot covers 4+ months of subscription

## Channels (Priority Order)

### 1. LinkedIn (Free, Highest Conversion)
- Personalized connection requests to NC DSCR LOs
- Value-first DM sequence (no pitch until DM #3)
- Share sample dossier PDF in conversation
- Target: 200 connections/month, 30-50 conversations, 5-10 calls

### 2. In-Person Meetups (Free, Highest Trust)
- REI meetups in Raleigh, Charlotte, Durham, Chapel Hill
- REIA chapter meetings
- LOs attend these to network with investors — you're offering them investors
- Bring printed one-pagers, swap contact info, follow up same night on LinkedIn

### 3. Cold Email (Near-Free, Scale)
- 3-email sequence from separate domain (protect stillmindcreative.com)
- Domain: buy dscrleads.co or similar (~$12/year)
- Zoho Mail free tier for sending
- Warm domain 5-7 days before first campaign
- Physical address + unsubscribe in every email (CAN-SPAM)
- Target: 100-150 emails/month

### 4. Facebook Groups (Free, Discovery)
- NC Real Estate Investor groups
- Raleigh/Charlotte REI communities
- Don't spam — answer questions, share insights, DM people who engage
