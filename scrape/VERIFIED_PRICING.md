## DNC Compliance

### Federal DNC Registry (FTC)
- **First 5 area codes**: FREE
- **Per area code**: $82/year
- **All area codes nationwide**: $22,626/year
- **For our use**: We only need South Florida (561, 954, 305, 786) = FREE (under 5)
- **Process**: Register as telemarketer at telemarketing.donotcall.gov, download numbers, scrub our list

### State of Florida DNC
- Florida has its own DNC list separate from federal
- Must comply with BOTH federal and state

### Free DNC Scrub Tools
- **FreeDNCList.com**: Upload CSV, free for 1 file per session, $19.99/yr for unlimited
- **EVS7.com**: Free DNC scrubber tool
- **Datazapp Phone Scrub**: $0.005/number with NO minimum (can use our $75 balance)

### DNC Compliance Requirements for Our Use Case
1. **Must scrub ALL phone numbers** against federal + state DNC before any outreach
2. **Mortgage solicitation is NOT exempt** from DNC rules
3. **Fines**: Up to $51,744 per violation (per call)
4. **Safe approach**: Use Datazapp phone scrub ($0.005/number) or FreeDNCList.com (free)
