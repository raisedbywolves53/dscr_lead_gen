## 5. REGULATORY LANDSCAPE

### Federal: Business-Purpose Exemption
- DSCR loans structured as "business-purpose" under TILA → **exempt from**:
  - Ability-to-Repay / QM rules
  - TRID disclosure requirements
  - HMDA reporting (mostly)
- CFPB oversight minimal for business-purpose loans
- No NMLS MLO licensing required in most states for business-purpose

### State-Level Considerations
- CA, NY, NJ, MD, OR, NV, UT require specific business-purpose lender licensing
- NY has 16% criminal usury for business loans <$2.5M
- Judicial foreclosure states (NY, NJ, FL, IL, OH) have higher loss severities → affects pricing
- STR municipal regulations create underwriting complexity

### Emerging Regulatory Risks
1. CFPB "business purpose" scrutiny under future administrations
2. State fair lending examinations
3. Municipal STR bans impairing collateral
4. Potential investor concentration limits

## 6. PERFORMANCE DATA

### Delinquency Rates
| Metric | DSCR (Non-QM RMBS) | Conventional | Agency Investor |
|--------|-------------------|--------------|-----------------|
| 30+ Day DQ | ~4-7% | ~2-3% | ~3-4% |
| 60+ Day DQ | ~2.5-5% | ~1-1.5% | ~2-2.5% |
| 90+ Day DQ | ~2-4% | ~0.8-1.2% | ~1.5-2% |
| Loss Severity | ~25-40% | ~20-30% | ~25-35% |

### Vintage Performance
- **2020-2021**: Best performing (low rates, appreciation tailwinds, 90+ DQ 1.5-3%)
- **2022**: Highest stress (peak prices, rising rates, 90+ DQ 3-5%+)
- **2023**: Smaller volume, conservative underwriting, early performance strong (<2%)

### Key Performance Drivers
1. Origination DSCR >1.25x dramatically outperforms sub-1.0x
2. LTV: Strong inverse correlation (>75% = 2-3x default of <65%)
3. Experience: First-time investors default at 2-3x rate of experienced (5+ properties)
4. Property type: SFR best; 2-4 unit and condos modestly higher DQ
5. Geography: Non-judicial foreclosure states show 40-50% lower loss severity

## 8. PRODUCT TRENDS

### Current Growth Areas
1. **Short-Term Rental DSCR**: 15-25% of volume, up from <5% in 2020
2. **Interest-Only**: 40-60% of originations carry IO period
3. **Ground-Up Construction DSCR**: <5% but growing; projected stabilized DSCR underwriting
4. **Portfolio/Blanket DSCR**: Single loan for 5-100+ properties
5. **5-8 Unit DSCR**: Bridging residential and commercial
6. **40-Year Terms**: Increasingly common to improve cash flow metrics
7. **Foreign National DSCR**: Growing niche, especially FL (LatAm) and CA (Asian)
8. **Rate Buydowns**: Temporary/permanent to help qualification

## 9. FORWARD OUTLOOK

### Bull Case
- "Renter nation" thesis supports rental demand and DSCR fundamentals
- Institutional capital entering SFR/BTR drives financing demand
- Rate normalization improves cash-on-cash yields
- Non-QM RMBS maturation tightens spreads
- Product innovation expands addressable market

### Bear Case
- Sustained >7% rates compress DSCR ratios
- 10-15%+ Sun Belt home price correction stresses LTVs
- Rent growth reversal impairs performance
- CFPB expansion into business-purpose oversight
- STR regulatory crackdowns
- Insurance cost inflation (FL, CA, TX, LA up 30-100%+)
- Climate risk in key DSCR markets

### Base Case (2025-2027)
- DSCR volume: 15-25% CAGR → $55-70B annually by 2027
- #1 non-QM product by wide margin (>65% share)
- DSCR RMBS approaches $25-35B annually
- Lender consolidation: 2-3 dominant national platforms emerge
- Spread compression continues as asset class matures
