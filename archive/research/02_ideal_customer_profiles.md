## TIER 1 ICP DEEP DIVES

### ICP #1: BRRRR Strategy Investors

**Profile**: Buy distressed → Rehab → Rent → Refinance into DSCR → Repeat. Ages 30-50, sophisticated, networked, run as primary business with teams.

**Why DSCR Fits**:
- DSCR is THE permanent financing product after rehab completion
- Fast underwriting supports capital velocity (3-6 month refi cycle)
- Many lenders allow refi at appraised value after 3-6 months seasoning
- Entity lending for LLC-held properties
- Complex tax returns from rehab costs/depreciation make conventional impossible

**Typical Deal**: $150K-$400K loan (75% of ARV), 3-8 refis/year per active investor

**Volume**: ~200K-400K active practitioners, $30-45B addressable annually

**Where to Find**:
- BiggerPockets (BRRRR forum is epicenter; David Greene's book 500K+ sold)
- YouTube: BiggerPockets, Graham Stephan, Meet Kevin, Brandon Turner
- Hard money lenders (every maturing bridge loan = DSCR refi lead)
- Local REIAs, contractor networks, title companies

**Triggers**: Completed rehab + tenant in place, hard money loan maturing, insurance policy change from "vacant/renovation" to "landlord," listing property for rent post-renovation

---

### ICP #2: Buy-and-Hold Investors (5+ Properties)

**Profile**: 10-11M individual landlords in US. Target is the ~2-3M actively acquiring/refinancing. Median age 45-55, ~70% male, college-educated, $75K-$200K HHI before rental income.

**Why DSCR Fits**:
- Hit the Fannie/Freddie 10-property cap
- DTI ceiling after 4-10 conventional mortgages
- Aggressive depreciation = low AGI on tax returns despite strong cash flow
- Want LLC vesting for liability protection
- Faster closing (21-30 days vs 45-60 conventional)

**Typical Deal**: $150K-$500K per property, scaling from 2-3 toward 10+ properties, 1-3 loans/year

**Volume**: At avg $275K loan × 2-3M active, $550-825B addressable; DSCR captures 5-8% = **$27-66B**

**Where to Find**:
- BiggerPockets (2.5M+ members), Reddit r/realestateinvesting (600K+)
- REIAs (200+ chapters nationwide under National REIA)
- Podcasts: BiggerPockets, Rental Income Podcast, Real Estate Guys
- Property managers, investment-focused RE agents, CPAs handling Schedule E

**Triggers**: Hitting 4th or 10th conventional mortgage limit, LLC formation, searching "no income verification rental property loan," refinancing hard money/private loans

---

### ICP #3: Short-Term Rental (Airbnb/VRBO) Operators

**Profile**: 1.5-2M STR listings in US, ~800K-1M unique hosts. Target: ~300K-400K "professional" hosts (2+ listings). Range from hobbyist to 100+ unit operators.

**Why DSCR Fits**:
- DSCR lenders accept projected STR income (AirDNA, Rabbu)
- Conventional lenders won't count or heavily discount Airbnb income
- Handles seasonality via annualized projections or 12-month trailing averages
- Speed for time-sensitive vacation rental acquisitions
- Entity lending standard

**Typical Deal**: $200K-$1.5M loan (STR properties are higher-value), $40K-$200K+ gross revenue per property

**Volume**: 100K-150K professional acquisitions/year × $400K avg = **$40-60B demand; DSCR $8-15B**

**Where to Find**:
- Airbnb host forums, STR Wealth Facebook (50K+ members)
- AirDNA, Mashvisor, Rabbu users (actively scaling)
- Revenue management: PriceLabs, Beyond Pricing, Wheelhouse users
- PM software: Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify users
- VRMA conferences, Robuilt YouTube (700K+ subs)

**Triggers**: New Airbnb/VRBO listing, AirDNA account activity, bulk furnishing purchases, STR business license applications, searching "Airbnb investment property loan"

### ICP #4: Self-Employed Professionals with Complex Tax Returns

**Profile**: Business owners, consultants, physicians, attorneys, tech entrepreneurs. Earn $150K-$1M+ actual but show $60K on 1040 after deductions. ~16.5M self-employed in US, ~500K-750K active/prospective RE investors.

**Why DSCR Fits**:
- Tax return income vs actual income gap (the core pain point)
- Zero income documentation required
- Speed: eliminates 2-year tax return, CPA letter, P&L documentation cycle
- Portfolio diversification for high earners
- Privacy: don't want to share personal financials

**Typical Deal**: $250K-$1.5M per property, 1-2 per year, higher-value properties in primary/secondary markets

**Volume**: 200K active buyers/year × $400K avg = **$80B addressable; DSCR $8-12B**

**Where to Find**:
- CPA and tax advisor referrals (#1 channel — they see the problem firsthand)
- Financial advisors/wealth managers recommending RE diversification
- Professional associations: AMA, ADA, state bars, tech groups
- LinkedIn: "Founder," "Owner," "CEO," "Self-Employed," "Consultant"
- White Coat Investor (physicians), Bogleheads, franchise expos

**Triggers**: Denied conventional mortgage despite high net worth, asking CPA about rental property with low taxable income, business sale/liquidity event, searching "no income verification mortgage"

## TIER 2 ICP SUMMARIES

### ICP #5: 1031 Exchange Buyers
- **~100K-150K exchanges/year, $100-200B total value**
- Extreme time pressure (45/180-day deadlines) makes DSCR's speed critical
- Failed exchange = $100K+ capital gains tax hit → deal certainty is premium
- **Primary channel**: Qualified Intermediaries (QIs) — IPX1031, Asset Preservation, Accruit

### ICP #6: Foreign National Investors
- **$53B annual foreign purchases, ~$15-20B addressable for DSCR**
- Top nationalities: Canada, China, Mexico, India, Colombia, Brazil, UK
- DSCR often the ONLY financing option for non-US citizens
- Concentrated in Miami, NYC, LA, Houston, Orlando
- **Channels**: CIPS-designated agents (8K+), immigration attorneys, international RE portals

### ICP #7: High-Net-Worth Individuals
- **7.5M HNW households in US, ~200K-400K actively acquiring investment RE**
- Value privacy (no income docs), convenience, speed
- Drive demand for jumbo DSCR ($1M-$3M+)
- **Channels**: RIAs/wealth managers, family offices, turnkey providers (Roofstock), Tiger 21/YPO

### ICP #8: House Flippers Transitioning to Rentals
- **200K-300K active flippers, 20-30% selectively holding = 50K-90K**
- Natural bridge-to-DSCR pipeline from hard money
- Accelerated by 2023-2025 flip margin compression
- **Primary channel**: Hard money lenders (the #1 referral source)

## TIER 3 ICP SUMMARIES

### ICP #9: Funds and Syndicators
- 5K-10K active syndication groups, $200-400B AUM
- Need portfolio/blanket DSCR products ($2M-$50M)
- **Channels**: SEC EDGAR Form D filings, CrowdStreet, Best Ever Conference

### ICP #10: Build-to-Rent Developers
- $30-50B annual BTR market, 100K+ units under construction
- Need construction-to-perm DSCR and portfolio programs
- **Channels**: NAHB, IMN SFR Forum, land brokers

### ICP #11: Corporate Housing Operators
- $4-5B market, 15K-25K operators; healthcare traveler boom driving growth
- 30-50% above-market rents = strong DSCR ratios
- **Channels**: CHPA, Furnished Finder, healthcare staffing agencies

### ICP #12: Real Estate Agents as Investors
- 1.5M agents, ~350K-450K own investment property
- **Dual value**: Direct client + 5-20 investor client referrals/year
- Commission income (1099, variable) makes conventional difficult
- **Channels**: NAR events, local boards, Keller Williams/eXp offices

## CROSS-ICP CHANNEL EFFICIENCY

These channels reach multiple ICPs simultaneously:

1. **BiggerPockets** — ICPs 1,2,3,5,7,8,12,15,17 (single most efficient channel)
2. **Hard Money Lender Referral Partnerships** — ICPs 1,8,12,15 (natural bridge-to-DSCR pipeline)
3. **CPAs/Tax Advisors** — ICPs 2,4,5,9,10,14 (trusted advisor referral)
4. **Real Estate Agent Partnerships** — ICPs 2,6,7,9,10,12 (transaction-level referral)
5. **Qualified Intermediaries** — ICP 5 exclusively but very high conversion
6. **NARPM/Property Management** — ICPs 2,13,14 (portfolio-level relationships)
7. **Turnkey Providers** — ICPs 4,7,16 (passive investor channel)
8. **Social Media (YouTube, IG, TikTok)** — ICPs 1,3,8,12,16,17 (awareness/education)
